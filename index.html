Loid Forger - The Spy

Loid Forger, also known by his codename "Twilight," is a highly skilled and suave secret agent who works for an intelligence agency. Loid is known for his exceptional espionage skills, which include disguise and combat abilities.

He is tasked with a mission to infiltrate a prestigious school to get close to a target, but to do so, he must create a fake family, leading to various comical and heartwarming situations as he tries to balance his spy life with his new role as a husband and father. Loid Forger is a complex character with a strong sense of duty, and he undergoes significant character development throughout the series as he navigates the challenges of his double life.

Anya Forger - The Telepath

Anya Forger is a young girl who is initially introduced as Loid Forger's supposed daughter in their fabricated family. Anya possesses a remarkable psychic ability that allows her to read people's thoughts by touching their skin, a skill that proves to be highly valuable in espionage work. She is a sweet and innocent child, often curious and eager to bond with her new family.

Throughout the series, Anya's character development is central to the story, as she begins to form a genuine bond with her fake mother and father, Loid and Yor Forger, and experiences the challenges and joys of a normal family life. Her journey as a pivotal character in "SPY×FAMILY" involves learning to navigate her unique abilities and the complexities of her dual identity, adding depth and heart to the series.

Yor Forger - The Assassin

Yor Forger is the wife of Loid Forger, the main character, and plays a central role in their fabricated family. Yor is an elegant and graceful woman who is initially unaware of her husband's secret life as a spy.

As the story unfolds, Yor gradually becomes aware of Loid's espionage work and the true nature of their family. Despite her initial naivety, she adapts quickly to the challenges of their double life and takes on the role of a loving mother to Anya, the girl they've taken in as their daughter. Yor's character development in the series revolves around her growth as a spouse and mother, as well as her willingness to support her husband and daughter in their mission, adding depth and complexity to her character.

Bond Forger - The Prognosticator

Bond Forger is the pet of the Forger family, and is also known as Subject 8. Bond is a large dog that was used for experiments which gave him the power to see the future. He is Anya's best companion, and frequently follows her adventures.

Throughout the story, Bond uses his abilities to help people in danger and even prevent deaths. He is petty and jealous, but apologizes when he realizes his actions upset Anya. Due to his past as a lab experiment, Bond is unfamiliar to regular dog activities. After joining the Forger family, Bond becomes grateful for having a normal life, and aims to protect them.
